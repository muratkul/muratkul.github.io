## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I scan a document? Does it work without a camera?
There are two ways: **Scan with Camera** (on devices with a camera) or **Choose Photo**. When you choose a photo, you can set the document's four corners (auto-detected, adjustable by hand); the perspective is corrected into a clean PDF. This path needs no camera — for example, you can pick a document photo you took on your iPhone and scan it on your Mac.

### I purchased Premium but the features are still locked
Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID, then tap **"Restore purchase"** on the paywall screen. If the issue persists, restart your device and try again.

### Is Premium a one-time purchase or a subscription?
It's a one-time purchase. You pay once and merging, splitting, and page reordering unlock permanently. There is no subscription.

### Where are the PDFs I create saved?
After creating a PDF, use **Save** to store it in the Files app or iCloud Drive on your device, or **Share** to send it to other apps.

### Are my documents safe?
Yes. PDF Converter works entirely on your device. No documents, photos, or data are sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### How do I change the app language?
Tap the **gear (Settings)** icon at the top-right of the home screen → choose Turkish or English under **Language**. The language changes instantly, independent of your device language.

### Can I create a PDF from another app?
Yes. In Photos or another app, select one or more images and use **Share → "Convert to PDF"** to create a PDF directly.
