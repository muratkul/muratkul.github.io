## Frequently Asked Questions

**Where are my notes stored?**
On your device, and — if iCloud is enabled — in your own iCloud account. We never see your
notes and there's no account to create. See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy).

**My notes aren't syncing between devices.**
Make sure iCloud Drive is turned on for Monkey Remembers in *Settings → [your name] → iCloud*,
and that both devices are signed into the same Apple ID and connected to the internet.

**Voice dictation isn't working.**
Voice dictation needs a real device with a microphone (it won't work in the Simulator). The
first time you use it, allow Microphone and Speech Recognition access when prompted. You can
review these under *Settings → Monkey Remembers*.

**An alarm didn't go off.**
Make sure notifications are allowed in *Settings → Monkey Remembers → Notifications*, and that
the alarm time is in the future. Reminders are delivered as local notifications.

**Is the app free? Do you collect any data?**
Yes, it's free, and we don't collect your data. See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy) and
[Terms of Service](./terms-of-service).
