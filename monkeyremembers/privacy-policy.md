# Privacy Policy

🌐 **English** · [Türkçe](./privacy-policy-tr)

_Last updated: June 3, 2026_

## 1. Introduction

Monkey Remembers: Notes, Tasks ("the app", "we", "us") is a simple note-taking and
reminder app for iPhone. This policy explains, in plain language, how the app handles
your information.

The short version: your notes stay on your device and in your own iCloud account. We
don't run servers that store your notes, and we can't read them.

Questions? Contact us at nfvwzhbrbw@privaterelay.appleid.com.

## 2. Data We Collect

We do not collect your notes. Everything you write or dictate is stored **locally on your
device**, and — if you have iCloud turned on — in **your own iCloud account**.

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or have access to any of your note content. There is no
account to create and no sign-in.

## 3. iCloud Sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device, the app keeps your notes in sync across your devices
using Apple's iCloud infrastructure. This data lives in your personal iCloud account and is
governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We never see or
have access to this data. If iCloud is turned off, the app works fully offline and your notes
stay only on your device.

## 4. Microphone & Speech Recognition

When you create a note by voice, the app uses Apple's speech recognition (SFSpeechRecognizer)
to turn your speech into text. Depending on your device and settings, this happens on-device
or via Apple's servers, and is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
The microphone is only used while you are actively dictating a note. We do not record, store,
or transmit audio ourselves.

## 5. Notifications

Alarms are delivered as **local notifications** scheduled on your device. No data is sent to
any server to make notifications work.

## 6. Copying & Sharing

When you choose to copy a note, its text is placed on your device's clipboard. When you choose
to share a note, its text is handed to the standard iOS share sheet so you can send it to
another app you pick (e.g. Messages or Mail). These actions happen only when you tap Copy or
Share, and where the content goes from there is governed by the apps and services you select.
We don't intercept, store, or transmit this content ourselves.

## 7. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track you
across apps or websites. There are no ad identifiers and no profiling.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13.
Since we don't collect data at all, there is nothing for us to gather about children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above and include the new
version with an app update. Significant changes will be highlighted in the app's release notes.

## 10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, email us at nfvwzhbrbw@privaterelay.appleid.com.
